Selection conclusion
- The MA strategy is the cleaner lower-turnover baseline.
- The Regime strategy achieves the stronger return profile on the current local dataset.
- SPREAD_TH = 0.0010 remains the selected robust regime setting.

Why SPREAD_TH = 0.0010 was selected
- SPREAD_TH was the only tested tuning lever that materially improved the regime strategy on this dataset.
- SPREAD_TH = 0.0009 sometimes looked stronger in a single run, but robustness was worse:
  - mean_return ≈ 0.05328369
  - mean_trades ≈ 71.7
  - one negative seed
- Nearby settings such as 0.00105 and 0.0011 also produced at least one negative seed.
- Therefore 0.0010 gave the best robust balance among the tested candidates.

Important implementation fixes reflected in these results
- preserved last nonzero signal across neutral bars
- canceled residual remainder after partial fills
- tightened breakout confirmation in RegimeAwareStrategy
- forced final flatten so runs finish flat cleanly
- SHOW_PLOTS toggle supports quiet testing and plot generation on demand
